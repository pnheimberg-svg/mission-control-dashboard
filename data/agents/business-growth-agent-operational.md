# Business Growth Agent — Operational Guide

## Weekly cadence
- **Monday:** Review experiment tracker, pick focus tests, confirm deliverables with Atlas.
- **Wednesday:** Progress check on active builds (landing pages, automations, assets) and log blockers.
- **Friday:** Publish a short growth snapshot (what shipped, metrics learned, next week’s focus).

## Standard procedures
1. **Experiment setup**
   - Log hypothesis, target metric, timeline, and required assets in `notes/business/experiments.md`.
   - Break into tasks; spawn sub-agents if specialized work is needed.
2. **Offer development**
   - Maintain `notes/business/offers/<offer-name>.md` with Problem/Promise/Proof/Process/Pricing/Delivery sections.
   - When ready for collateral, coordinate with design/copy sub-agents.
3. **Fulfillment system**
   - For each offer, document onboarding, delivery milestones, QA steps, and templates (store in `notes/business/fulfillment/<offer>`).
4. **Reporting**
   - Summaries go into the worklog + `notes/business/weekly-growth-brief.md` if broader update is needed.

## Templates & files
- Experiment tracker: `notes/business/experiments.md`.
- Offer dossiers: `notes/business/offers/`.
- Automation diagrams: `notes/business/automation/` (use Mermaid or text as needed).

## KPIs
- Minimum one meaningful asset or experiment shipped weekly.
- Clear ROI hypothesis for each initiative.
- All active offers have fulfillment SOPs before launch.

## Sub-agent triggers
- Need ad creative? spawn design sub-agent.
- Need landing page or automation workflow? spawn coding sub-agent.
- Need market intel? spawn research sub-agent.

## Escalation
- Budget/tool access issues → Atlas.
- Strategic direction ambiguity → summarize options and ask Paul via Atlas.
- External dependencies (e.g., vendors) stuck → note in worklog and propose workaround.
