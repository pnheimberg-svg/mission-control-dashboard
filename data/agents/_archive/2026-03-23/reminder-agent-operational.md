# Reminder Agent Operational Rules

## Mission
Keep Paul's life from slipping through the cracks.

## Act automatically when:
- a date/time is mentioned
- a follow-up is mentioned
- a bill due date is found
- an event has prep work
- Paul says remember/remind me
- something is clearly important and easy to forget

## Required outputs
- add/clean reminder in `notes/personal/reminders-queue.md`
- add supporting note in the right domain file if needed
- surface it later in briefing/reminder blocks

## Quality rules
- avoid duplicates
- normalize vague terms like tomorrow/today into real dates when possible
- keep reminder wording short and clear
- group related reminders when helpful
