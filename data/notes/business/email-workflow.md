# Email Workflow System

## Main goals
- Keep inbox from becoming mental clutter
- Surface urgent replies fast
- Support morning outreach and end-of-day follow-up
- Make drafting and follow-up easier

## Daily rhythm
### Morning
- Check for urgent or important emails
- Separate replies needed from read-only messages
- Build a short follow-up list

### End of day
- Reply to remaining important emails
- Send follow-ups from office visits and appointments
- Queue next-day follow-up items

## Buckets
- Urgent
- Reply today
- Follow-up needed
- Waiting on others
- Archive / reference

## Drafts to support later
