## Section 1 – Market Snapshot
- House painting & decorating revenue ≈ **$28.2B (2025, IBISWorld)** — plenty of crews can pay $750+ for a fast fix.
- ~27% of small businesses have no website; ~70% lack strong CTAs (LeadsAgent + MarketingLTB, 2025). Many painters fall here.
- Proof: Arrowhead Painting hit $70k/mo with 11 painters. Paul already built working sites for Expressions Painting and Pioneer Painting.

## Section 2 – Ideal Buyer
- Owner-led crews with **3–12 painters**, $15k–$120k/mo revenue, owner still answers every call.
- Weak Wix/Weebly site but present on Google Maps.
- Pain: referral-only calls, lost quote requests, no tracking.
- Trigger: lost bid, slow season surprise, newly hired salesperson.
- Buying story: want someone who “gets painting,” keeps it simple, handles the heavy lift.

## Section 3 – Value Equation Check
- Dream outcome: “Booked jobs and proof my marketing works.”
- Perceived likelihood: build the site before the call, show it live, reference Arrowhead/Expressions/Pioneer wins.
- Time delay: site + follow-up stack live in 5 days; blitz starts on day 5.
- Effort/sacrifice: owner only sends logo, services, and photos.

## Section 4 – Grand Slam Offer
**Pitch:** “I build your new painter site before we even talk. If you like it, it’s $750 to launch + $90/mo for hosting, instant text follow-up, and lead tracking. If you don’t see 10 new leads in 60 days, I keep working free.”

**Day 1–10 deliverables:**
1. Free spec site (logo, colors, service areas) before meeting.
2. Launch kit: copy, forms, SMS/email follow-up, GA, review link, Control Center tab.
3. Proof stack: crew bios, OSHA checklist, HOA/commercial PDF, gallery.
4. Google cleanup: verify GMB, 2 new posts, fix directories (Yelp, Angi, Houzz).
5. Fill-the-Calendar Blitz: 14-day outbound plan with scripts.

**Fast-decision bonus (48h):** $100K Lunch-and-Learn Playbook.

**Guarantee:** 10 net-new leads in 60 days or keep working free.

**Pricing ladder:**
- Base: $750 setup + $90/mo (site + automations + reporting).
- Growth: $1,250 setup + $150/mo (Base + quarterly content day + review pushes).
- Crew Club: $2,000 setup + $350/mo (Growth + ads coaching + AI follow-up system). Anchor Crew Club, sell Growth.

## Section 5 – Day-1 Message + Scripts
**Hooks:**
- “I built you a brand new site. Want to see it?”
- “I ran Arrowhead Painting to $70k/mo. I know where leads die.”

**Text template:**
> “Hey <Name>, Paul here (PaintUp), Fatu told me to reach out: I built you a brand new site—took me 2 hours. Want to see it? If you like it, it’s $750 to launch + $90/mo for hosting + instant text follow-up. If we don’t add 15 leads in 60 days, I keep working free.”

**Objection replies:**
- “We already have a site.” → Share Loom of upgraded version built for them.
- “No time.” → “You only approve copy once—I pulled everything else from old bids.”
- “Cash is tight.” → Split setup 50/50 or credit part toward referral.

## Section 6 – Day-1 Lead Plan (100M Leads Inspired)
**Week 1 (Owned):**
1. DM 10 painters you know with selfie video + spec site.
2. Ask Sherwin/Kelly reps for 3 intros each.
3. Call Expressions + Pioneer for two referrals (trade for free review push).

**Weeks 2–4 (Earned):**
1. Weekly posts in local FB/LinkedIn groups (“Painter Site Stress Test”).
2. 3-min YouTube Short: “The $1,500 leak hiding in most painter websites.”

**After 3 wins (Paid):**
1. $20/day Google LSAs for “painter website” (Stockton/Sac).
2. Mail 2-postcard set to C-33 licensees (CSLB list).
3. Retarget visitors with $5/day Meta ads showing spec reels.

**Scoreboard:** Contacts → Audits Booked → Spec Delivered → Closed → 60-Day Win (update daily).

## Section 7 – Product Build & Delivery (10-Day Sprint)
1. **Day 0:** Book meeting.
2. **Day 1:** Pull assets, build spec demo.
3. **Day 2:** Sales call showcasing demo.
4. **Day 3:** Close + collect $750.
5. **Day 4:** Finalize copy, embed forms, hook automations.
6. **Day 5:** Review with owner (30 min).
7. **Day 6:** SEO basics + GMB + directory fixes.
8. **Day 7:** Publish, connect domain, test automations.
9. **Day 8:** Train owner via Loom + control sheet.
10. **Day 9:** Launch Fill-the-Calendar Blitz.
11. **Day 10:** Schedule 30-day KPI sync.

## Section 8 – Money Model & Capacity
- Target CAC < $400 (warm intros dominate).
- Setup gross margin ≥ 70% (template + AI copy).
- Monthly gross margin ≈ 80% (hosting/automation <$20).
- Capacity: 4 active builds/mo solo (batch copy/design/launch days).
- Upsell path: Growth tier (reviews/content) → Crew Club (ads coaching + AI follow-up).

## Section 9 – Metrics & Proof
Track weekly: audits booked, launches live, leads captured per client, revenue added. Collect screenshots (texts, dashboards) for proof deck.
