# Church Retention and Re-Engagement System

## Main insight
If everyone came at once, the church would already be around 50.
So the problem is not only new visitors. It is consistency, sticking, and re-engagement.

## Main goals
- Notice people drifting earlier
- Follow up intentionally
- Help people feel known, connected, and needed
- Create simple next steps for people to stay engaged

## Buckets
- Consistent core people
- Inconsistent attendees
- Missing lately
- New but promising
- Needs personal follow-up

## Re-engagement ideas
- Personal check-in text
- Friendly call
- Invite to specific upcoming service or event
- Ask if anything is going on / offer prayer
- Clear next step to reconnect

## Questions to answer later

- Who can help follow up besides you?
- What kinds of touchpoints make people feel connected fastest?
- Which people are most at risk of drifting right now?
