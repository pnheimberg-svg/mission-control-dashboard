# Facilities Expo Follow-Up System

## Goal
Turn expo conversations into real follow-up instead of lost contacts.

## Same-day workflow
After the expo, capture each contact into one of these buckets:
- Hot follow-up
- Good fit / follow up this week
- Long-term / nurture
- Vendor / connection
- Not a fit

## Minimum info to capture
- Name
- Company
- Role
- Phone
- Email
- Type of facility/company
- What they seemed to care about
- Next step
- Follow-up timing

## Recommended follow-up timing
### Hot follow-up
- Same day or next morning
- Short thank-you + clear next step

### Good fit
- Within 48 hours
- Thank-you + reminder of what you do + offer to connect

### Long-term
- Within 3-5 days
- Light relationship follow-up

## Suggested next-step tags
- send intro email
- call
- drop by office
- connect on LinkedIn
- send estimate info
- add to nurture list

## Fast logging format
- Name / Company:
- Need / Pain point:
- Next step:
- Follow up by:

## Post-expo priority order
1. People with active facility needs
2. Property/facility managers
3. Strong relationship opportunities
4. Everyone else worth keeping
