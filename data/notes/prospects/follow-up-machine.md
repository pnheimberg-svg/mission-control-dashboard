# Prospect Follow-Up Machine

## Main use
Help Paul keep momentum after office visits, estimates, appointments, and prospecting.

## Daily buckets
- Offices visited today
- Follow up tonight
- Call tomorrow
- Far-away prospects for phone block
- Waiting on response

## Suggested daily flow
### During the day
- Log offices visited
- Log estimates / appointments
- Capture quick notes on decision-makers and next steps

### End of day
- Send follow-ups to offices visited
- Reply to email responses
- Build next-day call list
- Move long-distance prospects into call block

## Tracking format
- Company / office
- Contact
- Last touch
- Next action
- Notes
- Priority
