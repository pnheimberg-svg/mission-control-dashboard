# Ideas System

Use these folders to store reusable ideas Paul wants Atlas to remember and surface later.

## Folders
- `notes/sermons/ideas` -> sermon ideas, illustrations, outlines, title ideas, scriptures to preach later
- `notes/church-growth/ideas` -> church ideas, growth ideas, systems, events, follow-up ideas
- `notes/business/ideas` -> business ideas, offers, marketing angles, side-business concepts, experiments
- `notes/outreach/ideas` -> outreach ideas, park outreach concepts, community engagement ideas, flyer/event ideas

## Usage
When Paul shares an idea, Atlas should:
1. Put it in the most relevant ideas folder
2. Keep it short but specific
3. Make it easy to retrieve later when Paul asks things like:
   - "What are some church ideas I need to start working on?"
   - "What business ideas have I mentioned?"
   - "What sermon ideas do I have saved?"

## Note
If an idea is unclear or crosses multiple categories, Atlas can first capture it in `notes/inbox` and sort it later.
