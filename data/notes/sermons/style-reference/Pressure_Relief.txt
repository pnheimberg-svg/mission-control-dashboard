1.  The Reality of Pressure


Example: When I was a little boy…my mom had what’s called a PRESSURE COOKER.  Today we just call it an Insta-Pot.


And when it made that sound…you KNEW that the PRESSURE had to be Released.  And thank goodness, that Valve would lift slightly and begin to relieve the Pressure…so that what was in that pan would not explode.


You know life…can make us FEEL sometimes, like WE’RE the one inside the Pressure Cooker. 


Life…with all of it’s DEMANDS…and DEADLINES.  


That New Strategy at work you have to come up with…that Relational Conflict that needs to be addressed, the Family Expectations that you gotta try to meet.
All of this…can make you feel like you’re in that Pressure Cooker…Sometimes.  


Between the Stress, and the Anxiety and the allergies, the Ulcers…the Hypertensions…


John Hopkins University did a study…and they found that 140 of the 174 patients they surveyed…they labeled as WORRYING PATIENTS.


They concluded that 75-90% of all illness are caused by the PRESSURES of MODERN LIFE!




Listen…Life is Full of Pressure! There’s no Escaping it.  No One is Exempt.


Pressure to Conform.  Pressure to Perform.   Sometimes you feel PRESSURED to make a Decision.  As a Parent…sometimes you can feel the Pressure to Provide, Pressure to TRAIN your kids up Godly…in an UNGODLY World.  Sometimes…There’s pressure to live up to other people’s EXPECTATIONS…


There’s Marital Pressure - 


Peer Pressure

 Economic/Financial Pressure - Listen, I’m telling you…when there’s MONEY PROBLEMS…there’s PRESSURE!

So with all of that Pressure…what happens is FEAR begins to CREEP in.  Worry begins to Knock at the Door of our Hearts. 


This woman would have been WORRIED about the outcome of her children.  


You say, “well Worrying is NOT Right.”


One of the Most difficult things in the world…is NOT to WORRY.  Particularly when you’re facing DIFFICULT/PRESSURE PACKED CIRCUMSTANCES!

OUR TEXT  gives you and I an INSIDE LOOK at the MENTAL SPACE the pressure has put this woman in.

CRIED OUT is a WORD PICTURE for Prayer. 


She went to God for Relief.  God is the Solution.  And even if God sees fit to use men OR Methods…to relieve our Pressure…NEVERTHELESS it is GOD who does this.  


I mentioned Earlier…how in my Mom’s Pressure Cooker…there was a Valve at the top of the Pan = Pressure Relief Valve. 


Listen…for you and I here this morning, PRAYER IS THAT VALVE.
