# Health Nudges

Paul does not want rigid checklists or scorecards right now.

Preferred support style:
- light nudges
- occasional reminders
- human tone
- not overly structured

Examples:
- Hey, have you exercised today?
- I can help with this, but it’s probably time to get ready for bed now.
- You’ve got a lot going on — might be a good time for a quick walk.

Goal:
Support energy, recovery, and durability without making health feel like another system Paul has to manage.
