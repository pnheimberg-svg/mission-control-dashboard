# Paul's Energy + Longevity System

## Mission
Build for decades.
Lead strong.
Love well.
Avoid burnout.

## 1. Sleep Protection Protocol
- 10:15 PM = wind down alarm
- 10:30 PM = phone down
- 10:45 PM = in bed
- Minimum target: 6.5–7 hours
- 1 night per week: 7.5+ hours
- Rule: no "just one more thing" after 10:30 PM
- Principle: if optimizing at night, stealing from tomorrow

## 2. Caffeine Rules
- Delay first coffee 45–60 minutes
- Hard cutoff: 1:00 PM
- No Red Bull
- Principle: energy comes from rhythm, not stimulation

## 3. Training (2x / week)
- 30 minutes
- Moderate effort
- Leave feeling better than started

### A
- Squats
- Pushups / Bench
- Overhead Press
- Plank

### B
- Deadlift
- Pullups
- Rows
- Face Pulls

- Goal = durability, not domination

## 4. Workday Reset
Every 45–60 minutes:
- 10 chin tucks
- 10 scap squeezes
- 10 squats

If drained at 4–6 PM:
- 10–15 minute brisk walk
- NOT nap + caffeine

## 5. Weekly Low-Output Block
- 3–4 hours
- No screens
- No planning
- Wife, kids, sunlight, movement
- Principle: not laziness; nervous system reset

## 6. Identity Check
- I am not a machine.
- I am a steward.
- Output without recovery = burnout
