# Paul's Daily Rhythm

## Monday-Friday
- Wake up at 6
- Shower
- Coffee
- Pray
- Head to work while listening to Bible or podcasts
- Start work:
  - Email sending / follow up
  - Prospecting
  - Office visits / estimates / appointments
- End of day:
  - Reply to emails
  - Send follow-ups to offices visited
  - Call new prospects farther away than he can drive to
- Head home while listening to a podcast
- Relax for a little bit
- Work on sermons / church work
- Dinner with family
- Hang out with wife
- Pray
- Sleep

## Saturday-Sunday
- Outreach
- Church events

## Health overlay
- Protect sleep: wind down 10:15 PM, phone down 10:30 PM, in bed 10:45 PM
- Delay first coffee 45–60 minutes
- No caffeine after 1:00 PM
- Use workday reset every 45–60 minutes
- If drained 4–6 PM, brisk walk instead of nap + caffeine
